# Calendar

The Calendar view lays tasks out on a grid by their due dates.

## Views

- **Week**: tasks spread across the seven days of the current week
- **Month**: tasks shown as dots on a monthly calendar

## Navigation

- Use the **Previous** and **Next** arrows to move between weeks or months
- Click **Today** to jump back to the current period

## Filtering

The Calendar supports the same filters as the Tasks view:
- Area
- Device
- Priority

## Task dots

Tasks appear as colored dots on their due date. The dot color corresponds to the task's priority level:

| Priority | Dot color |
|---|---|
| Low | Green |
| Medium | Yellow |
| High | Orange |
| Critical | Red |

Click on a dot or a day to see the tasks due on that date.
